Dining

Savories

  1. Spinach and Artichoke Dip - The perfect topping for our complimentary Westfall Bread Sticks, this warm dip elevates the texture and taste of the already delicious bread by adding a creamy flavor (5g)

  2. Garden Cucumber Finger Sandwiches - Two small sandwiches with soft white bread filled with cream cheese and cucumbers from the chateau's own vegetable garden, mixed with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and garnished with fresh chives. (10g)

  3. North Sea Crab Cakes - Two golden brown cakes made with North Sea crab meat and a tasteful red sauce, garnished with parsley and served with tartar sauce. (25g)

  4. Kun Lai Chicken Spring Rolls - Two spring rolls inspired by Kun Lai, made with fresh chicken, greens from the chateau's own vegetable garden, and served with soy sauce. (10g)

  5. Charcuterie Board - This board contains a delectable combination of cheeses Darnassian Bleu, Redridge Roquefort, Alterac Swiss, and Dalaran Sharp alongside jellies made from fresh produce grown in Northshire, Eastvale, Teldrassil, and Elwynn. It is a classic tour de taste for members of the Alliance. (25g)

Entrees

  1. Beef Wellington - Delicious, rare red meat from Elwynn-raised cattle wrapped in a warm, crusty pastry that will make your mouth water. * (25g)

  2. Smoked Salmon - Freshly caught Lakeshire salmon smoked to perfection, blackened lightly, and served with a lemon wedge. * (20g)

  3. Lamb Lollipops - Rack of lamb ribs cooked medium rare with a delicious sauce. * (25g)

  4. Roasted Duck with Blackberry-Orange Sauce - Rich duck meat topped with a scrumptious and tangy sauce made from garden fresh blackberries and oranges (30g)

  5. Seared Scallops with Pomegranate Glaze - Fresh from the shores off Stormwind Harbor, scallops grilled to perfection and topped with a fruity glaze to add an additional zest to the already delicious dish (15g)

Vegan Entrees

  1. Garden Salad - Freshly grown vegetables from the chateau’s garden beautifully chopped and served into this full salad, which contains romaine lettuce, tomatoes, warm croutons, thinly sliced purple onions, and optional feta cheese and deviled egg with a classic dressing. * (10g)

  2. Spinach Salad - A slightly more upscale garden salad with spinach in place of romaine. (12g)

  3. Balsamic Chickpea Salad - Enjoy this zesty take on a salad that features chickpeas instead of lettuce. The chickpeas are mixed in a balsamic dressing and coated in avocados and feta cheese. * (15g)

  4. Cauliflower Steak with Hazelnut and Brown Butter - If you're a vegetarian or vegan who wants a filling meal, our hearty cauliflower steak, topped with roasted hazelnuts and a creamy brown butter, is the perfect substitute for animal-based alternatives. * (20g)

  5. Eggplant Parmesan - Eggplant grown in the chateau's own garden and topped with creamy parmesan cheese from Elwynn-raised cattle. * (15g)

Each Entree is served with your choice of two side dishes, not including the bread sticks, which are complimentary

Sides and Soups

  1. Breadsticks - Westfall-raised wheat baked beautifully into delicious breadsticks. (Complimentary)

  2. Garlic Green Beans - Fresh and delicious green beans marinated in garlic. (4g)

  3. Mashed Potatoes - Classic mashed potatoes from Westfall with all the trimmings, chives, butter, salt, and pepper. (4g)

  4. Macaroni and Brie with Crab - An elevated take on a popular dish, this macaroni salad is topped with fresh Elwynn brie and crab straight from the Stormwind Harbor (10g)

  5. Basil Soup - Tomatoes grown from the chateau’s own garden blended masterfully into this delicious and creamy red soup. (6g)

  6. Miso Soup - Traditional Pandaren miso blended with fresh Pandaren herbs. (7g)

Any salad can be ordered as a side dish as a substitute for another side or soup.

Desserts

  1. Assorted Macarons - Several small macarons fashioned to the shape of two budding rose petals, varying in lemon, vanilla, red velvet, and strawberry flavors. (10g)

  2. Strawberry Shortcake - Fluffy white cake served with strawberries and whipped cream. (10g)

  3. Parfaits - An assortment of parfaits included creamy peach, tangy lemon, creamy chocolate, lime crumble, zesty orange, melted white chocolate, and caramel. (15g)

  4. Turtle Cheesecake - Enjoy a delicious slice of freshly baked cheesecake topped with a caramel and chocolate drizzle, which can be removed by request to make this an ordinary yet delectable dessert. (15g)

  5. Cherry and Rose Eclairs - Two soft sandwiched pastries layered with cherry cream filling and rosewater. Garnished with edible, dried rose petals. (10g)

  6. Apple Tarts - Two modestly-sized sweet tarts filled with apples grown in Northshire Valley, topped with just a taste of cinnamon and powdered sugar. (5g)

  7. Dessert Tree - If you cannot decide between all of the delicious dessert options we have on offer, this dessert tree will offer you a little taste of everything! The tree comes with a lemon macron, a vanilla macron, a creamy chocolate parfait, one cherry and rose eclair, and one apple tart. (30g)
