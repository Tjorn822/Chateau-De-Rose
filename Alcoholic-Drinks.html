Cocktails

  1. Siren's Kiss - A classic from our partners at Lark & Siren Brewing Co., this cocktail is a mix of gin, lime juice, pomegranate juice, honey, sparkling water, grapefruit juice, and edible red pearl dust with a garnish of spring mint, pomegranate seeds, and orange peel curl served in a frosted glass. (20g)
