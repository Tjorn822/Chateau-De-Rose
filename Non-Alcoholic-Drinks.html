Non-Alcoholic Drinks

Standard Drinks and Coffee

  1. Water - Standard filtered, still water. (Complimentary)

  2. Sparkling Water - Fresh spring sparkling water from the Redridge Mountains. (5g)

  3. Milk - Creamy, delicious milk from Elwynn-raised cattle. (3g)

  4. Pink Lemonade - Tangy but sweet lemonade in a beautiful pink hue. (3g)

  5. Hot Chocolate Float - Two scoops of vanilla ice cream in a glass of hot chocolate and chocolate chips, garnished with whipped cream and chocolate shavings. (5g)

  6. Foggy Coffee - Regular coffee with exceptional beans grown in Zandalar served with an optional cool, refreshing side glass of milk and cream from Elwynn-raised cattle. (7.5g)

  7. Drustvar Dark Roast - A deep, dark coffee made from coffee beans grown in Drustvar, Kul Tiras. This strong coffee is only recommended for those who like their coffee strong. (7.5g)

  8. Espresso - No coffee list would be complete without the classic espresso. (5g)

Warm Teas

  1. Earl Gray Tea - The classic warm team for those who enjoy a little boost of caffeine with their tea, served with a lemon wedge. (5g)

  2. Honey Mint Tea - A warm, refreshing tea with hints of mint grown by the Rosewood Apothecary and a generous serving of honey. (3g)

  3. Special Draenor Blend - A blend of herbs grown on Draenor, Lady Rose's home world. This exotic tea is for those who seeks to take their taste buds on an interstellar journey. (8.5g)

Cold Teas

  1. Chilled Tea - The classic cool tea, served with a lemon wedge. (3g)

  2. Sweet Tea - A cold, sweet tea for those who like a little hint of sweet with their tea. (3g)

  3. Peach Tea - Chilled tea with the summery flavors of peach. (3g)
