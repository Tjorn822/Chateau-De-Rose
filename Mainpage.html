About Us

Chateau Rose Café is an upscale cafe and tavern open every Wednesday evening from 7:30 PM RT - 9:00 PM RT. Though the cafe may have white linen table cloths, people from all walks of life and every social class are welcome to attend the weekly openings for a great opportunity to socialize and network! Enjoy divine food and drinks, beautiful melodies from our professional bards, and top class service from our talented wait staff. You might even catch a special performance while you're there! The cafe is always looking to expand our roster of waiters, bards, barkeeps, and security staff, so if you are looking for work in one of those capacities, consider joining us for competitive pay and a friendly work environment!

Location

The chateau is located in Cathedral Square of Stormwind City; however, A Hero's Welcome in Dalaran (Legion) serves as our proxy location. You can access this Dalaran by using the hearthstone obtained in the Warlords of Draenor expansion, which is an easy and quick way to get to the tavern. For IC purposes, we are in Stormwind City and the chateau has no relation to either Dalaran or A Hero's Welcome.

The chateau is divided into three sections: the ground floor, the upper floor, and the beer garden. All three of these sections are fully serviced by the chateau and there should ideally be a member of wait staff in each of the sections. Any special performances will occur in the beer garden. The kitchen is included on the ground floor, though the kitchen should be inaccessible to patrons. There is an administrative office on the upper floor; however, the chateau does not extend into the Greymane Enclave area. Basically, if you enter the Greymane Enclave zone on your map, you've left the area serviced by the chateau.
